# Structured Content: Funlab Digital Transformation Infographic

## Title
Funlab's Salesforce Digital Transformation
*From Go-Live to 200% Revenue Growth*

## Learning Objectives
- Understand the 3-cloud Salesforce architecture powering 60+ entertainment venues
- See how an 8-step LWC booking wizard drove 200% online revenue growth
- Learn the integration surface: payments, tax, availability, contracts
- Appreciate the CI/CD discipline behind 26 months of continuous delivery

## Sections

### HERO CELL (2x2) — Key Outcomes
**Concept:** Results at a glance
**Content:**
- 200% online revenue growth
- 69% increase in online conversion rates
- 60+ venues · 3 regions (AU, NZ, US)
- 40% faster team onboarding
**Visual:** Large bold numbers with upward arrows; simple bar/growth chart
**Labels:** "200% Revenue Growth", "69% Conversion Lift", "60+ Venues", "40% Faster Onboarding"

### CELL 1 (2x1) — The Platform
**Concept:** 3 Salesforce clouds + integration landscape
**Content:**
- Sales Cloud — Opportunities, Quotes, function bookings
- Service Cloud — case management, guest enquiries, payment disputes
- Marketing Cloud — confirmation emails, automated guest comms, upsell journeys
**Visual:** Three cloud icons in a row with brand colour fills
**Labels:** "Sales Cloud", "Service Cloud", "Marketing Cloud"

### CELL 2 (1x2) — LWC Booking Wizard
**Concept:** 8-step wizard, event-driven architecture
**Content:**
- 8-step Lightning Web Component wizard
- 14+ sub-components
- Event-driven: no direct component coupling
- Steps: venue → date/time → packages → guests → pricing → payment → docs → confirm
**Visual:** Vertical step flow or numbered progress bar
**Labels:** "8-Step Wizard", "14+ Sub-Components", "Event-Driven Architecture"

- Conga Composer + Conga Sign — contracts
**Visual:** 4 integration icons with connecting lines
**Labels:** "Funhouse API", "AvaTax", "Stripe / AakPay", "Conga"
